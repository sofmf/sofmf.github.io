Objavljanje dogodkov

Dogodki, ki jih organizira Študentska organizacija FMF, bodo objavljeni z zamudo. To pomeni, da bodo informacije o preteklih dogodkih na voljo naknadno, kar omogoča vpogled v aktivnosti organizacije in daje študentom možnost, da spremljajo in analizirajo izvedene projekte ter dogodke.

Event Publication

Events organized by the Student Organization of FMF will be published with a delay. This means that information about past events will be made available retroactively, allowing students to review the organization's activities and track the implementation of projects and events.
