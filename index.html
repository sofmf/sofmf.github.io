Študentska organizacija FMF (SO FMF)

Študentska organizacija Fakultete za matematiko in fiziko (SO FMF) je del Študentske organizacije Univerze v Ljubljani (ŠOU v Ljubljani), ki letno razpolaga s približno 5 milijoni evrov. SO FMF je izvoljena struktura, katere predstavnike študenti volijo na dve leti. Trenutno ima na voljo proračun v višini 1.200 evrov mesečno, ki ga namenja za organizacijo dogodkov, projektov in pobud v korist študentov fakultete. Glavna naloga SO FMF je zastopanje interesov študentov FMF in izboljšanje njihovega študijskega ter obštudijskega življenja. Organizacija pripravlja akademske in družabne dogodke, kot so strokovne delavnice, predavanja, študentske konference, športne aktivnosti in družabna srečanja. Poleg tega sodeluje s fakulteto in drugimi organizacijami pri izboljšanju študijskih pogojev ter reševanju problemov, s katerimi se soočajo študenti. V Študentskem zboru ŠOU v Ljubljani ima FMF dva poslanca, ki zastopata interese študentov naše fakultete na širši ravni in sodelujeta pri sprejemanju pomembnih odločitev za vse študente Univerze v Ljubljani.

Student Organization of FMF (SO FMF)

The Student Organization of the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics (SO FMF) is part of the Student Organization of the University of Ljubljana (ŠOU v Ljubljani), which manages an annual budget of approximately 5 million euros. SO FMF is an elected body, with representatives chosen by students every two years. Currently, it has a monthly budget of 1,200 euros, which is allocated for organizing events, projects, and initiatives that benefit the students of the faculty. The primary role of SO FMF is to represent the interests of FMF students and enhance their academic and extracurricular experience. The organization hosts academic and social events, such as professional workshops, lectures, student conferences, sports activities, and social gatherings. Additionally, it collaborates with the faculty and other organizations to improve study conditions and address student-related issues. FMF has two representatives in the Student Assembly of ŠOU v Ljubljani, who advocate for the interests of our faculty’s students at a broader level and participate in making important decisions affecting all students at the University of Ljubljana.
